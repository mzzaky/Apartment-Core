Overview

Core Feature

The Research System is a progression feature that allows players to conduct scientific research and unlock permanent buffs that apply to all their apartments. These buffs can significantly enhance income generation, reduce costs, and provide other strategic advantages.

✓ Key Features
  • 6 Unique Research Types - Each provides different strategic benefits
  • 5 Tiers Per Research - Progress from Tier I to Tier V for maximum bonuses
  • Permanent Buffs - Research completed once, benefits last forever
  • Visual Progress Tracking - GUI shows all research progress at a glance
  • Achievement Integration - Earn achievements for completing research milestones
💡 Information
The research system is fully integrated with other apartment features including income generation, tax calculations, auction house, and apartment ownership limits. All buffs are automatically applied whenever relevant calculations are performed.

Tiers & Progression

Mechanic

Each research type has 5 tiers (I, II, III, IV, V). Players must complete each tier before moving to the next. The buffs stack additively - having Tier III means you get 3x the base effect.

Tier Visual Representation

In the Research GUI, tiers are displayed using visual indicators:

  • █ (Green) - Completed tier
  • ▒ (Yellow) - Currently researching
  • ░ (Dark Gray) - Not yet started

Tier Progression Example - Revenue Acceleration

Tier Income Interval Reduction Cost Duration
I -5% 50,000 1 hour
II -10% 150,000 2.5 hours
III -15% 450,000 6.25 hours
IV -20% 1,350,000 15.6 hours
V -25% 4,050,000 39 hours

Total Investment for Full Research

To complete all 5 tiers of a single research, players must invest approximately 6,050,000 in costs and approximately 64 hours of research time.

Rules & Mechanics

Guide

Basic Rules

  • One Active Research - Players can only have one research running at a time
  • Upfront Payment - Research costs are deducted immediately when starting
  • No Cancellation - Once started, research cannot be cancelled (no refunds)
  • Permanent Benefits - Completed research buffs never expire
  • Independent Research - Each research type can be upgraded independently
  • Stackable Buffs - All research buffs stack additively

Research Process Flow

1 Select Research

4 Start Timer

Validation Checks

Before starting a research, the system performs these validation checks:

  1. System Enabled - Research system must be enabled in configuration
  2. No Active Research - Player must not have another research in progress
  3. Not Max Tier - The selected research must not already be at maximum tier
  4. Sufficient Funds - Player must have enough money to pay for the research
⚠ Important Notes
  • Research continues even when the player is offline
  • Data is saved persistently - research progress is not lost on server restart
  • Buffs are applied automatically in all relevant calculations

Cost & Duration

Formula

Research costs and durations scale exponentially with each tier. This ensures that higher tiers require significantly more investment but provide proportionally greater benefits.

Cost Formula

Cost = Base Cost × (Multiplier)^(Tier - 1)

Duration Formula

Duration = Base Duration × (Multiplier)^(Tier - 1)

Default Values by Research Type

Research Type Base Cost Cost Multiplier Base Duration Duration Multiplier
Revenue Acceleration 50,000 3.0x 3,600 sec (1 hr) 2.5x
Capital Growth 75,000 3.0x 5,400 sec (1.5 hr) 2.5x
Tax Efficiency 100,000 3.5x 7,200 sec (2 hr) 2.5x
Expansion Plan 150,000 4.0x 10,800 sec (3 hr) 3.0x
Auction Efficiency 80,000 3.0x 5,400 sec (1.5 hr) 2.5x
Vault Expansion 250,000 4.5x 21,600 sec (6 hr) 3.5x

Example Calculation - Vault Expansion

For Tier III Vault Expansion (Base: 250,000, Multiplier: 4.5):

  • Cost: 250,000 × 4.5^(3-1) = 250,000 × 20.25 = 5,062,500
  • Duration: 21,600 × 3.5^(3-1) = 21,600 × 12.25 = 264,600 seconds (~73.5 hours)

Configuration

Setup

The research system is configured through the research.yml file. This file controls all aspects of research costs, durations, effects, and GUI settings.

Configuration File Location

plugins/ApartmentCore/research.yml

Main Configuration Options

research:
  enabled: true  # Enable/disable research system
  max-concurrent: 1  # Max researches per player (future use)

Per-Research Configuration

researches:
  revenue_acceleration:
    display-name: "Revenue Acceleration"
    description: "Reduces income generation interval by 5% per tier"
    icon: "CLOCK"
    effect-per-tier: 5.0
    cost-base: 50000
    cost-multiplier: 3.0
    duration-base: 3600
    duration-multiplier: 2.5

Auction Efficiency (Special Case)

Auction Efficiency has two separate effect values - one for fee reduction and one for commission reduction:

auction_efficiency:
    effect-per-tier-fee: 5.0    # Fee reduction %
    effect-per-tier-commission: 1.0  # Commission reduction %

GUI Configuration

gui:
  title: "&8Research Center"
  size: 54
  border-material: "PURPLE_STAINED_GLASS_PANE"
💡 Configuration Reload
The research configuration can be reloaded using the /apartment reload command or by restarting the server. Changes to the configuration file will take effect after a reload.

GUI Interface

Interface

The Research Center GUI provides an intuitive interface for managing all research activities. Players can view their progress, start new research, and monitor active research all from one screen.

GUI Layout (54 slots)

  • Slot 4 - Info display (Enchanting Table) showing overall progress and active buffs
  • Slots 10, 12, 14, 16, 21, 23 - Research type items
  • Slot 31 - Active Research display (shows progress bar and time remaining)
  • Slot 49 - Back button to return to main menu
  • Border Slots - Purple stained glass pane decoration

Research Item Display

Each research item in the GUI displays:

  • Name - Research type name with tier indicator
  • Description - What the research does
  • Tier Progress Bar - Visual representation of completed tiers
  • Current Buff - Active benefit from completed tiers
  • Next Tier Info - Cost, duration, and buff for next tier
  • Status - Available, researching, maxed, or insufficient funds

Implementation

Technical

The research system is implemented using several Java classes that work together to provide a seamless experience.

Core Classes

  • ResearchType.java - Enum defining all research types with their properties
  • PlayerResearchData.java - Data class storing player research progress
  • ResearchManager.java - Main manager class handling all research logic
  • ResearchGUI.java - GUI implementation for the research interface

Data Persistence

Player research data is stored in:

plugins/ApartmentCore/data/research_data.yml

Data structure:

players:
  :
    completed:
      revenue_acceleration: 3
      capital_growth: 2
      tax_efficiency: 0
      expansion_plan: 1
      capacity_expansion: 0
      auction_efficiency: 4
    active:
      type: "tax_efficiency"
      tier: 1
      start-time: 1234567890000
      duration: 3600000

Tick Processing

The research system uses a Bukkit scheduled task that runs every 20 ticks (1 second) to check for completed researches:

// Runs every 1 second
tickTask = plugin.getServer().getScheduler()
    .runTaskTimer(plugin, new Runnable() {
        public void run() {
            // Check each player's active research
            for (PlayerResearchData data : playerData.values()) {
                if (data.isResearchComplete()) {
                    completeResearch(playerId, data);
                }
            }
        }
    }, 20L, 20L);
💡 Offline Progress
Since the tick task runs server-side, research continues to progress even when the player is offline. When they next log in, any completed researches will already be applied.

Achievements

Integration

The Research System integrates with the Achievement system to reward players for their research progress.

Research Master Achievement

Players can earn the RESEARCH_MASTER achievement by completing research at maximum tier. The achievement tracks how many researches have reached Tier V.

Achievement Requirement Description
Research Novice 1 max-tier research Complete any research at Tier V
Research Apprentice 2 max-tier researches Complete 2 researches at Tier V
Research Expert 3 max-tier researches Complete 3 researches at Tier V
Research Master 4 max-tier researches Complete 4 researches at Tier V
Research Legend 5 max-tier researches Complete 5 researches at Tier V
Research Grandmaster 6 max-tier researches Complete all researches at Tier V
✓ Achievement Integration
When a research completes, the system automatically checks if any research has reached maximum tier and updates the player's achievement progress accordingly. No additional action is required from the player.
