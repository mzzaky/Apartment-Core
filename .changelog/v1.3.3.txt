=== Research System (New Feature) ===

- Added a new Research System that provides permanent player-level buffs through tier-based research (Tier I-V).
- Created `research.yml` configuration file with configurable cost (exponential scaling), duration (exponential scaling), and effect values per research type.
- Created `data/research_data.yml` for persistent storage of player research progress and active research state.
- Implemented 5 research types:
  1. "Revenue Acceleration" — Reduces income generation interval by 5% per tier (applied as proportional income boost).
  2. "Capital Growth Strategy" — Increases income generation amount by 5% per tier.
  3. "Tax Efficiency Strategy" — Reduces final tax amount by 5% per tier (stacks with shop tax reduction).
  4. "Expansion Plan" — Increases apartment ownership limit by +1 per tier.
  5. "Auction Efficiency" — Reduces auction creation fee by 5% per tier and lowers commission rate by 1% per tier.
- Research costs scale exponentially: cost = base * multiplier^(tier-1). Default bases range from $50,000 to $150,000.
- Research durations scale exponentially: duration = base * multiplier^(tier-1). Default bases range from 1h to 3h.
- Players can only run one research at a time; completion is checked every second.
- On research completion: player receives chat notification, title message, and sound effect.
